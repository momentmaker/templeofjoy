I have been a seeker of truth my entire life. I found Yoga in my early 20's and dove into the philosophy. Over the years, I read many books on yoga, but didn't come across Autobiography of a Yogi until 2014. At that time, I had a yoga studio and was meditating regularly. One of my friends asked me about the book and since I hadn't read it yet, she gave me a copy. As soon as I started reading it, I was hooked! My life has not been the same since. I have been a dedicated disciple ever since.
