# Future Ideas

Ideas brainstormed during initial design. Not in v1 scope, saved for later.

## Seeds of Joy Board
A space for ideas that aren't offerings yet. "I want to start a kirtan circle in Portland." Other devotees can respond and collaborate. Where new joy gets born.

## Seva Exchange
Gift economy exchanges between devotees. "I'll build your website if you teach my daughter harmonium." No money. Pure selfless service aligned with Yogananda's teachings.

## Path Timeline
Optional visual timeline on each profile showing key moments in a devotee's spiritual journey. Not a resume -- a story of awakening.

## QR Codes for Retreats
Every listing gets a printable QR code for sharing at retreats, gatherings, and satsangs. Physical-digital bridge that spreads Temple of Joy organically.

## "This Week in Joy" Newsletter
Not a list of submissions -- a curated moment. One devotee's story, one quote, one offering. Three minutes to read. Something people forward to friends.

## Community Constellation
A visualization page showing the community as a constellation of stars. Each devotee is a point of light. Hover to see name and offering. Zoom by region. Evokes the feeling of a worldwide spiritual family.

## Annual "Year of Joy" Report
Every December, a curated page celebrating the year: growth, new devotees, countries reached, stories that moved us most.

## Meditation Mode
Dark mode reframed as "Meditation Mode." Calm, reduced stimulation, subtle spiritual motifs. On-brand alternative to standard dark mode.

## Map View
Interactive globe/map showing devotee locations worldwide. The "whoa" moment that makes the community's reach visible.

## Collaboration Board
Devotees seeking partners for spiritual projects. More structured than Seeds of Joy.

## Mentorship Matching
Connecting newer seekers with experienced devotees who opted in to mentor.

## Multilingual Support
Hindi, Italian (large Ananda community), Spanish, and other languages for global inclusion.

## "I Know This Person" Vouching
A trust signal on profiles. Visitors can click "I know [Name]" and submit a brief note ("We practice together at Ananda Austin"). Creates a GitHub issue for review. Once approved, profile shows "Vouched for by N community members." Not reviews or ratings -- quiet trust from real connections.

## "This Week in Joy" Auto-Newsletter
GitHub Action on a weekly cron. Pulls latest listings, picks a random Joy in Action story, formats a beautiful email, sends via listmonk. Zero manual effort. The community gets a weekly heartbeat.

## Devotee Count Badge on Homepage
Dynamic count at build time: "N devotees across M countries." Makes the community feel real and growing. Every merge updates the number.

## Auto-Generated Profile Share Cards
Personalized OG images per devotee for social sharing. When someone shares their profile URL, the preview shows their name, avatar color, location, and tags instead of the generic site image.
